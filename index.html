Extracurriculars

Boulder Colorado has become my home the last four years and being involved and connected with the Boulder community has always been a very important goal of mine. To do this I have consistently volunteered to give back and spend time with the community I appreciate and love so much.

Sports have heavily influenced who I am today, teaching me invaluable lessons and creating exceptional connections. Throughout my four years here I have been a member of the CU Boulder FreeSki Team, and have also been apart of Intramural soccer, football, and volleyball teams.

Growing up, I've always had a passion for cars and everything related to them. I've been drawn to hands-on projects, and often ones that cause more trouble than good. However, they've taught me patience and resilience from a young age.

That same passion carries over into electronics, where I enjoy fixing power tools or building and upgrading PCs. In the end, both have helped me develop a growth mindset, which has prepared me well for this career path.
